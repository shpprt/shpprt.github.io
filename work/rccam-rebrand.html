RCCAM Rebrand

As a student worker of both the Richard and Carole Cocks Art Museum and the University Communications and Marketing department, I was able to work cross-functionally to achieve a unified brand across multiple mediums. Design files were made accessible and clean, keeping them intuitive for future users and future revisions.


In the process of the rebrand, we recognized that communication between departments among the art museum was inefficient at sharing and organizing assets. We collectively developed a file structure utilizing Dropbox, Google Drive, and Asana.
