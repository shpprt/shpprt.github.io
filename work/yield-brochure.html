YIELD Brochure

Yield initiatives at Miami University confirm student admissions by highlighting the majors and minors offered. This brochure was sent to add first-year domestic undergraduate students who were admitted to Miami with a major within the College of Arts and Science.


Below were tasks outlined in Asana for designing and updating the YIELD brochure:


  • - Define scope of campaign/deliverables
  • - Define specs of deliverables
  • - Establish a timeline for deliverables
  • - Create a Google Drive folder for the campaign/deliverables
  • - Kick off project (meeting or email)
  • - Determine who needs to review

The collaborative process was similar to a client request to a design agency. The bulk of my work at UCM consisted of this kind of work. As a face-paced work environment, the first draft turnaround time was 1 day, and it took 2 weeks to complete after a few iterations. 20 thousand copies of this brochure were printed and distributed across the state.
