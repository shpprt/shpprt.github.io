Total Solar Eclipse

2024’s total solar eclipse took place over Miami University’s campus, and the Richard and Carole Cocks Art Museum took the opportunity to hold a watch party on the front lawn. There already existed marketing material for eclipse events around the city of Oxford, so I was tasked to convert the design into a motion graphic for the art museum to use.


The motivation I had for creating motion graphics propelled me to finish the project in only a week. I learned encrypted text effects and more advanced compositing in After Effects. When posted, the instagram reel received 50% more views than normal.


The city of Oxford saw my animation and requested an altered version of it with their solar eclipse related events to replace the art museum’s events. I learned a lot of things about after effects throughout the project, including the encrypted text effect and more advanced compositing.
