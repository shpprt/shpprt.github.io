Electric Root Festival

The Electric Root Festival promotes black culture and radical hospitality, intending to break down social barriers and offer a proposal to humanity for a more compassionate community. My team designed its branding and signage systems for 2023. After working and volunteering, I went to Tallahassee, Florida as a guest speaker for the National Civil Rights Conference to discuss radical hospitality as it relates to the Civil Rights movement.

​​In 2024, I was invited to create a new look for the branding. Our team members developed multiple iterations to go in a new direction, keeping the event fresh and engaging. Additionally, I helped create extra assets to be used for non-designers on Canva.
