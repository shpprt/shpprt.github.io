Board Game Day

My communication design major in college was rigid with little to no cohort mingling, so I took initiative and organized an event called Board Game Day to foster connection across cohorts. Board Game Day was an independent project that combined board games, food, and designers. I rented out a room and advertised the event across the art buildings. The event turnout was 38 people, including 10 seniors, 20 juniors, and 8 sophomores.


This was one of many events I ran independently. To make events like this more sustainable and frequent, I partnered with a friend to create a student organization called Design Collective, of which I am the Event Coordinator. We are continuing to grow our student group that will unite design cohorts beyond our graduation.
