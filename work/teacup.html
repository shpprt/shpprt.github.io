Teacup

I participated in a DTIYS (Draw This In Your Style) on Instagram as a way to stay involved with the art community. I modeled a teacup, cookies, and glass of milk in Blender, and then drew over it, exported the assets, placed them in the scene, and then drew over it again. Through this, I developed my skills in merging 2D and 3D assets together as well as 3D texturing.
