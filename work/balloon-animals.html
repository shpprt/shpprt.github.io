Balloon Animals

Through 9 juried Student Response Exhibitions over the past 11 years, the Richard and Carole Cocks Art Museum at Miami University has encouraged students to creatively reflect and respond through artistic expression for the community to engage with and interpret.


In 2023, the exhibition It's Our World was composed of 22 artists and 31 works. Students responded to our changing environment, illustrating how creative expression can be used to observe, understand, represent, and draw attention to environmental issues that face individuals and the collective in the one world we share.


Here is my response:


As the environment continues to suffer at the hands of humans, animals all over the world are forced to seek refuge in the last, untouched ecosystem: the sky. The adage "the sky's the limit", once a source of inspiration, now rings hollow for a future that seems hopelessly lost. The many creatures that dot the clouds capture the diversity of wildlife, highlighting nature's acceptance of all species.
